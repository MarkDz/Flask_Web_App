Welcome to Mark’s Tunes Music Player

Overview:

Mark’s Tunes Music Player is a great way to listen to your favorite music. The player can output most of the popular formats. It can be customized and set to play multiple playlist. Dashboard is the application main screen it features Playlist Name and Options. In the options you can click to play, click to edit or delete Playlist. Click to play will take you to the player screen where you can control your player. The player uses standard features like play, stop, move to the next song, back to previous song, valume control, shuffle, continues play and full screen. To add playlist click on Add Playlist link in the upper right corner. To create new playlist just insert the name in the Playlist Name bar and press Save Playlist. The new playlist will appear in your dashboard. You can also add the playlist from dashboard using Add Playlist button. To add songs to your playlist click on click to edit link. From there you can choose from the list of available songs. To add the song to your playlist just click on Save to Playlist. When done adding songs click on Back to Playlists to see your newly created playlist. To delete playlist in your dashboard just click Delete Playlist the playlist will update instantly. To log off just click Log Off in the upper right corner. I hope you will enjoy Mark’s Tunes Music Player.
